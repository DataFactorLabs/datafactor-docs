# Company Overview

DataFactor (operating as ZeroClick) is the reasoning-time advertising infrastructure 
for AI products. We enable AI platforms to monetize through contextually relevant, 
non-intrusive advertiser placements that surface alongside model responses — 
when user intent is highest and most commercially meaningful.

## Philosophy
AI monetization should align with user intent, not compete with it. 
No dark patterns. No degraded free tiers. No interstitials.

## Partners
DataFactor powers monetization for leading AI consumer products, 
including OnePrompt — the multi-model AI comparison platform.
